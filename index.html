Shillong Cricket Association

The Shillong Cricket Association (SCA) is a premier governing body and a key affiliate of the Meghalaya Cricket Association. Established as a cornerstone of regional sports, the SCA manages the competitive Shillong Cricket League, including its prestigious Super Division. Based at the iconic Polo Ground, the association plays a vital role in grassroots development and talent scouting across East Khasi Hills. By organizing structured tournaments for various age groups, the SCA remains instrumental in nurturing cricketers for state and national representation.

Shillong

The cradle of cricket in the hills

Shillong is widely regarded as the cradle of cricket in Meghalaya, possessing a rich heritage that dates back to the British era when the game was first introduced to the Khasi Hills. As the state’s primary cricketing hub, the city’s iconic Polo Ground serves as the spiritual home for the sport, hosting the historic Shillong Cricket League and fostering a deep-seated club culture. This high-altitude capital has consistently acted as the nerve center for talent identification, producing the state's most decorated players and officials who paved the way for Meghalaya’s full membership in the BCCI. Today, Shillong remains the vital link between grassroots passion and professional excellence, sustaining the sport through its vibrant local divisions and dedicated coaching academies.
