Our Legacy

Shillong Cricket Association

The Shillong Cricket Association (SCA) stands as the pioneer of organized cricket in the state of Meghalaya. Established to nurture the immense sporting talent within the East Khasi Hills district, the SCA has evolved from a local sporting body into a vital affiliate of the Meghalaya Cricket Association (MCA).

Historical Roots

Cricket in Shillong dates back to the British era at the historic Polo Grounds. The SCA was formalised to provide a structured league system, ensuring that the passion for the "gentleman's game" translated into professional opportunities for local youth.

Our Mission

To promote, develop, and regulate the game of cricket in Shillong by providing state-of-the-art infrastructure, transparent selection processes, and high-performance coaching for all age groups.

Today, the SCA manages multiple divisions, including the prestigious Super Division League, which features the top clubs from across the region. We take pride in our grassroots scouting programs that identify young talent from schools and rural belts, preparing them for state and national representation under the BCCI umbrella.
